Need help?

Here's a quick guide...

First of all, you need to log in or create an account.

Once loged in you'll see your existing tasks in the main page if you have any. You can also see them in your profile.

In your profile you'll see some profile data along with your current tasks and a few stadistics.

In your profile you have a section where you can see your accomplished tasks.

To add a new task you just have to click in the middle button in the navegation bar that says "Add new task".
